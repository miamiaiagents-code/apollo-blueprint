### 4. How It Works — "Three workflows that run without you."
Subtitle: "Automated intelligence loops that keep you informed and the business moving — every day, every week."

**The Morning Brief (8:00 AM ET · Daily)**
1. Maya — Pulls Shopify revenue, Meta ROAS, TikTok ROAS from last 24 hours
2. Fin — Pulls overnight P&L signals, margin alerts, cash position
3. Kira — Pulls top-performing creator content and new creator applications
4. Cleo — Pulls overnight review alerts and top support issues
5. Chain — Pulls inventory alerts, stockout warnings, shipment updates
6. Apollo — Compiles everything into one Telegram message to Hudson. 8am, every day.

**The Creative Flywheel (Every Monday · Weekly)**
1. Maya — Sends Pixel last week's top-performing ad creatives by ROAS and CTR
2. Cleo — Sends Pixel top review phrases and recurring customer language
3. Kira — Sends Pixel which creator content performed best in paid amplification
4. Pulse — Sends Pixel channel-level performance data and creative fatigue signals
5. Pixel — Synthesizes all inputs into the Weekly Creative Brief — hooks, angles, formats, AI tool recs

**The Numbers Review (Every Friday · Weekly)**
1. Maya — Compiles week's revenue, ROAS, and subscription metrics
2. Fin — Prepares weekly P&L summary — revenue vs target, margin vs budget, cash position
3. Globe — International market entry progress — UK, AU, CA pipeline status
4. Vault — Retail buildout progress — Mall of America, American Dream Mall status
5. Apollo — Sends Hudson a Friday digest — financials, growth, expansion. One message.
